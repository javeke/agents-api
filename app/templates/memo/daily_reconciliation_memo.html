Daily Settlement Reconciliation Workflow

  1. Receipt of VisaNet Settlement Report
    WiPay receives a daily email from JMMB with the attached VisaNet Settlement Summary Report. This report outlines:
    • Total settlement amount (USD and/or TTD)
    • Number of processed transactions
    • Interchange values, reimbursement fees, and net amounts
    • No transaction-level breakdown is included in the summary.
  2. Internal Review of FAC Transaction Report
    WiPay performs reconciliation using our FAC system report. This report covers a 24-hour period:

    {{ fac_time_window }}

    It includes transaction-level data to verify:
    • Total amount processed
    • Transaction count
    • Settlement time alignment
  3. Reconciliation & Matching
    The Finance team compares the VisaNet summary against the FAC report to validate:
    • Total transaction value
    • Total transaction count
    • Applicable fees
    • Overall settlement accuracy
  4. Discrepancy Management
    Any discrepancies are flagged and reviewed. WiPay will reach out to JMMB for clarification and resolution.

This memo and the attached reports aim to strengthen coordination and ensure mutual understanding between both teams. We look forward to continued collaboration in maintaining a seamless settlement process.
